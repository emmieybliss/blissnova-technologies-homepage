Who We Are

Building the Digital Future of Africa

BlissNova Technologies is a Lagos-based technology company building next-generation digital platforms, SaaS tools, content ecosystems, and marketplace products engineered for Africa's rapidly expanding internet economy.

From biography and entertainment media to domain trading and digital asset marketplaces, we create infrastructure that empowers Nigerian creators, entrepreneurs, and businesses to thrive online — with or without Western gatekeepers.
